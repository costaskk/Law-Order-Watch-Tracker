# v2.5 Actor detail interaction

- Actor cards in episode detail popups are now clickable.
- Clicking an actor closes the popup, selects that actor in the regular actor filter, clears show/season/status restrictions, and shows their matching episode list.
- Actor portrait previews appear on hover/focus when profile artwork exists in the generated cast index or episode cast metadata.
- Episode detail visibility, artwork tiles, cast cards, and modal contrast were polished.
